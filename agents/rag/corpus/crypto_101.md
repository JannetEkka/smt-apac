# Crypto 101 — the assets behind SMT

**Crypto** is digital money and digital assets recorded on a public ledger called a **blockchain**.
Anyone can verify the ledger; no single bank controls it.

**The pairs SMT watches.** SMT trades eight liquid crypto pairs: BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA,
DOGE, LTC. A "pair" like BTC means Bitcoin priced against a stable dollar-like unit.

**On-chain data.** Because the ledger is public, you can see large transfers and how value flows
between wallets. SMT's whale and on-chain personas read these signals — money moving is a clue.

**Volatility.** Crypto moves fast, up and down. That's risk *and* opportunity. SMT's job is to
decide when the odds are good enough to act, and to be honest when they aren't.

**Sentiment.** News and social mood push prices. SMT's sentiment persona reads the tone (via
Gemini) so the crowd's emotion is one input among many — never the whole story.
