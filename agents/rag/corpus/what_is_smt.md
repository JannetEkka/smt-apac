# What is SMT?

SMT (Smart Money Trading) is a transparent AI that makes a market call **and shows its
reasoning**. Instead of one black-box model, SMT runs six specialist "personas" — order-flow,
technical, on-chain/whale, sentiment, and market-regime — and a JUDGE that weighs them into one
decision: LONG (bet it goes up), SHORT (bet it goes down), or WAIT (sit out).

The point that makes SMT different: every decision comes with a faithful, three-sentence **why** —
the call, who actually drove it, and what the dissenters thought. You never have to trust a number
you can't see behind.

## How to read a decision
- **Action** — LONG / SHORT / WAIT.
- **Conviction %** — how sure SMT is. Below its floor, SMT WAITs rather than force a trade.
- **Risk score** — a 0–100 beginner-friendly read; sitting out is low risk.
- **Drivers** — the personas that led the call.
- **The why** — plain English, no jargon.
